GUIDELINES

1.Document Collection:The customer provides necessary documents such as government-issued ID and proof of address.

2.Document Submission:The customer takes live photo and saves it for next step.

3.Verification:The submitted documents are verified by the service provider for authenticity and accuracy, either manually or using automated software.

4.Biometric Verification :Facial recognition is required for enhanced security.

5.Compliance Checks:The service provider conducts compliance checks to ensure the customer meets regulatory requirements, including screening against sanctions lists and PEP databases

6.Approval:Once the KYC process is successfully completed and all necessary checks are passed, the customer's account or application is approved, granting access to the requested services.
